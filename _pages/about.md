I am a fifth-year Ph.D. scholar at the IIT Madras advised by [Prof. B. Ravindran](https://dsai.iitm.ac.in/~ravi/) and [Prof. Nirav P. Bhatt](https://scholar.google.co.in/citations?user=b6dXpnAAAAAJ). My research works focus on learning safe policies by infering safety from non-preferred (unsafe) demonstrations. I am broadly intersted in Reinforcement Learning and its application to robotics. I am also interested in building safe robotic systems that can generalize across multiple tasks communicated via natural language or visual cues, in unseen environments.
